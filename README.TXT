USER INSTRUCTIONS:

Click a piece then click another location or piece to move or take

Rules:

There are two side in chinese chess, Black and Red. same as international chess, who ever takes other's king, who wins.

帅/将 - this is the king in chinese chess, who ever got taken, who loses. Can only move one step at a time, only vertically or horizontally. Only in it's 2 by 2 territory.
士/仕 - this is the only piece in chinese chess that can move diagonally, only one step at a time can only move diagonally. with in the 2 by 2(path are drawn)
相/象 - this piece can only moves in a 2 by 2 path, kind like this 田, but only inside the border. and it can't move when there's a piece in the center of the 2 by 2
R    - this piece moves kind like knight. but it move's 2 by 1. when there's a piece line up with it, it can't move to that direction
/e - moves as same as rook
炮/包    - moves as same as rook ,but when taking a piece, it can only jump over one to take the piece.
兵/卒 - can only move one step at a time, after it crosses border then it can move horizontally. cannot move bake.
